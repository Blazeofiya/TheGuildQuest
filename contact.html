Frequently Asked Questions

What is a DIG?

A Decentralized Impact Guild (DIG) is a community group focused on local action, authentic connection, and mutual support. DIGs operate without hierarchy, focusing instead on showing up consistently and contributing unique skills.

How do I join APS Delray?

Simply show up to our next gathering! There's no formal application process or membership fee. Check our events section for details on our upcoming meetup at Atlantic Dunes Park.

Do I need special skills to participate?

Not at all. The only requirement is your authentic presence. Everyone has something valuable to contribute, whether it's a skill, perspective, or simply showing up consistently.

Is there a cost to join?

No. DIGs operate on principles of gift and contribution rather than financial exchange. Occasionally we might collectively contribute to a project or meal, but there are never membership fees.

Can I start a guild if I don't live near Delray?

Absolutely! We encourage guild formation anywhere people want to build authentic community. Check our "Start a Guild" page for guidance, and reach out if you need support.

Ready to Join the Movement?

The journey begins with simply showing up. We hope to see you at our next gathering!
